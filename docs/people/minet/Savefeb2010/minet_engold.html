    • Research Topics :

  • I am interested in the quality of service in networks
    (e.g. mobile ad-hoc networks, wireless networks)
    and in distributed real-time systems
    (e.g. multimedia systems, distributed systems with persistent objects,
    replicated databases).

    • My work consists in designing algorithms, proving their properties and establishing their feasibility conditions.
    I study more particularly the quality of service aspects dealing with:
    - real-time constraints (e.g.: tasks termination deadlines, messages delivery deadlines,
    end-to-end jitter),
    - fault tolerance (e.g. : type and number of processor/network failures tolerated in a given time interval),
    - and consistency constraints.

  • I am also interested in concepts and algorithms
    making easier the introduction of new services,
    or improving the performance of existing services in networks
    (e.g. mobility management by means of mobile ad-hoc networks techniques,
    optimized broadcast in a wireless multi-hop network).
